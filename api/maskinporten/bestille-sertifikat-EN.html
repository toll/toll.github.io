Order a certificate

To authenticate your business with Maskinporten you need a certificate. The certificate proves the identity of the business, and is used to sign the token request to Maskinporten.

Does the business have a Norwegian organisation number?

This determines both which certificate you need and how you order it.

  • Choose Norwegian business if the business has a Norwegian organisation number in Enhetsregisteret. If the business uses a NUF but lacks a Norwegian representative able to log in to Samarbeidsportalen, you should choose 'Foreign business'.
  • Choose Foreign business if the business does not have a Norwegian organisation number, or if it lacks a Norwegian representative able to log in to Samarbeidsportalen.
